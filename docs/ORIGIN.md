# Origin

RFP Response Assembler exists after the scorecards, trust-room, and procurement-answer layers.

The goal is to turn that executive-intelligence estate into one explicit buyer-response surface:

- which response packs are ready to reuse now
- where coverage or differentiation is still too weak
- what evidence is strong enough to support a buyer-safe RFP story
- which routes still need cleaner ownership and packaging before they can convert in procurement or legal review
